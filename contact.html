Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common queries regarding our quoting process and services.

Email Another Question
Do you charge for quotes or site visits?
No, all our site visits and quotations are provided completely free of charge with no obligation.
How quickly can you start a project?
This depends on our current schedule and the size of the job. However, we always aim to book you in as soon as possible and will provide a clear timeline during your consultation.
Are you fully insured?
Yes, Buchanan Landscapes holds full public liability insurance to give you complete peace of mind while we work on your property.
Do I need to be home while you work?
Not necessarily. As long as we have access to the garden and any required water/power points, we can complete the work while you are out. We are trustworthy and respectful of your property.
What happens if it rains?
We monitor the weather closely. Light rain won't stop us, but heavy downpours may require us to reschedule certain tasks (like laying patio pointing) to ensure a high-quality finish. We will always communicate any changes promptly.
Can you source specific plants or materials for me?
Yes, we work with trusted local suppliers and nurseries to source high-quality materials, turf, and plants specific to your design requirements.
