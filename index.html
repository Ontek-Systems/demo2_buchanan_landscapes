Common Questions

Got Questions?

Find quick answers to common queries about our landscaping and maintenance services.

Ask Another Question
What areas do you cover?
We are based in Surrey and proudly serve the surrounding areas. Contact us to see if we can reach your property!
Do you offer regular garden maintenance?
Yes, we provide ongoing garden maintenance tailored to your needs, whether that's weekly, fortnightly, or monthly visits.
Are your quotes free?
Absolutely. We provide free, no-obligation quotes for all potential projects, from minor hedge cutting to major patio installations.
How long does a new patio installation take?
The timeline varies based on the size and complexity of the design, but most standard patios take between 3 to 7 days to complete.
Do you dispose of the garden waste?
Yes, all green waste and debris created during our work is fully cleared and responsibly disposed of, leaving your property spotless.
Can you repair damaged fences?
We offer both complete new fencing installations and repairs to existing structures, ensuring your property remains secure.
When is the best time to lay new turf?
While turf can be laid year-round, autumn and early spring are typically the best seasons as the cooler, wetter weather helps the roots establish.
Is your pressure washing safe for all driveways?
We adjust our jet washing pressure and techniques depending on the surface—whether it's block paving, concrete, or natural stone—to clean effectively without causing damage.
